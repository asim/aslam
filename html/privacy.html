Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

What we collect

When you create an account, we store your email address and name. If you sign in with Google, we receive your email and name from Google — nothing else.

Your chats, notes, and reading progress are stored on our servers so you can access them across devices.

If you grant location permission, we use your coordinates solely to calculate prayer times. Your location is stored on your account and is not shared.

How we use it

Your data is used only to provide the service — showing your chats, notes, prayer times, and reading progress. We do not sell, share, or monetise your data.

When you ask a question in chat, your message is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that processing.

What we don't do

  • No tracking or analytics
  • No advertising
  • No selling of data
  • No third-party cookies

Data storage

All data is stored in an encrypted database on our servers. Content is private by default — only you can see your chats and notes unless you explicitly make them public.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual chats and notes at any time. To delete your account entirely, contact us and we will remove all your data.

Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@aslam.org
